About the Event

The Community Fest is an annual celebration bringing together residents from all walks of life. Our aim is to create a platform for local talent, provide entertainment for all age groups, and strengthen community bonds.

From live music performances to engaging workshops, there's something for everyone. Come and be a part of this unforgettable day!
